📖 Our Story

About Island Tales

Where Caribbean heritage meets the magic of storytelling

Island Tales by Caribbean Scribbles is an interactive, AI-powered storytelling platform that brings the rich heritage of Saint Kitts and the wider Caribbean to life for children aged 3–14.

Founded by Venetta J. Smithen — author, publisher, and cultural advocate — Island Tales was born from a simple belief: every child deserves to see their culture reflected in the stories they read.

Originally prototyped on the Emergent Platform and now powered by Manus AI, Island Tales uses generative AI to turn traditional Caribbean legends into immersive, character-driven experiences — complete with Kittitian Creole narration, interactive quizzes, and age-adaptive content.

Four simple steps to a world of Caribbean adventure

🎯
